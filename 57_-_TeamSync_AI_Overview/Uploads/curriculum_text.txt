Comprehensive Soccer Training
Curriculum Blueprint (U6 to U14)
This blueprint contains a curriculum spanning U6 through U14, with age-scaled field
dimensions, player counts, grouping recommendations, and PvP classification for all drills.
Field Geometry: 120 units (Length) x 80 units (Width) (Master Field)

Training Areas)
All drills are conducted within these defined training areas, which are scaled versions of the
